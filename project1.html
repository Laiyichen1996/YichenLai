Cultural and Art Center With Concert Hall


Site: Gunan Street, Dingshu Town, Yixing City, Jiangsu, China
Status: Course Assignment, Individual Work
Time: 2018 Spring
Skill: Modeling, Imaging, Rendering
It represents my interpretation of the local region in China called “Jiangnan”. In the project, I combined the tile roofs in the painting, Village, of Mr. Wu Guanzhong, a famous artist in China, with the style of gardens in Jiangnan. With slanted roof in Jiangnan style, the size of every individual building is kept within an appropriate range, so they can be better integrated into the local environment. Also, people can appreciate the buildings in the balance between public and private areas thanks to the corridors which blur the boundaries between the rooms and the outside world. Besides the local characteristics, the steel-wood structure in the large-span concert hall and glass curtain walls allow people to have different experiences from traditional architectures. By doing so, my work can take into account new technologies while revitalizing regional characteristics. Project Resume Contact
Standing on the top of the mountain, like the long film of the movie, sweeping from the left to the right, the houses surrounded by mountains and rivers grow into an orderly level of texture, each of which looks like a connection. The roof looks like a continuous stretch, falling from each other, and there is a picturesque in the painting of Wu Guanzhong.
The building layout continues the directionality of the horizontal texture of the site, and the volumes on the north and east sides are reduced to fit the scale of the surrounding buildings.

A private path, surrounded by quiet residential areas, so the back of the public area, offices and studios are placed here.
Garden is an extension and complement of this public building, the living space of people is expanded through the semi-outdoor platforms on the first and second floors.
The traditional buildings around the site are made of wood, so the main material of the project is wood, which hopes to have the same affinity as traditional buildings. However, t he compression resistance of the wood column is poor, so the steel-wood structure is selected, and the column is steel, and the other members are in the form of wood.
