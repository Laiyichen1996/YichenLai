Hello, I’m Yichen Lai
I graduated from the Department of Architecture at Southeast University in China, and I am currently studying at the faculty of Architecture at the Delft University of Technology. I am inclined to sustainable development, advocate the use of reasonable advanced technology, support “multicultural” communities, and build professional values with a delicate balance between “regional” and “global”. As Paul Ricoeur said in his book “History and Truth”: “There is the paradox: how to become modern and to return to sources; how to revive an old, dormant civilization and take part in universal civilization.” This prompted me to focus on building a balance between local culture and universal culture in my architectural design projects.
